Who Am I?

My name is Haydn Fee and i'm a web and graphic designer located in London, Ontario. I studied in the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe and have been learning graphic design in my spare time for nearly two years.

What I Do

I am skilled in using photoshop and illustrator to create a wide variety of images, from website designs and assets creation to logos and photo manipulation. I also have experince with 3d modeling and animation via cinema 4D as well as front-end web development.
